About Elite Family Dental

Where Quality & Care Comes Together

Founded by Dr. Sanganand and Dr. Nithya Gavle, Elite Family Dental brings specialist-level endodontic and orthodontic care to a single family-run practice in Attapur, Hyderabad. Both doctors trained through Osmania Hospital's rigorous residency programs and built this clinic around one idea: every member of a household — from toddlers to grandparents — deserves the same standard of care in one trusted place.

The clinic combines hospital-grade sterilization, digital diagnostics and a calm, unhurried environment so that even first-time or anxious patients feel at ease.
