Our Story

Welcome to Elite Family Dental! Founded in 2019, we are a family-run dental practice proudly serving the Attapur community. As a husband-and-wife team, we combine professional expertise with a warm, personal touch. Our focus is completely on providing comfortable, judgment-free care for patients of every age. Whether it's your toddler's first checkup or a complex restorative procedure, we treat your family like our own.
