Our Story

Started at a kitchen table in 2019. Still run that way.

Dr. Sanganand and Dr. Nithya Gavle opened Elite Family Dental with a simple premise: patients deserve a dentist who knows their name, not a queue number. As a husband-and-wife team, they've built a practice where clinical precision meets an unmistakably personal touch.

Every treatment plan — from a child's first cleaning to a full root canal — is explained plainly, priced honestly, and paced to the patient, not the clock. That's the whole philosophy, and it hasn't changed since day one.
